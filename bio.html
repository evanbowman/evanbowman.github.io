Evan Bowman
Blog Portfolio Bio Contact

Evan Bowman was born in Fairfax, Virginia in 1995, and grew up in upstate New York. His earliest artistic influence came from his mother, a graphic designer and painter. He continued his art education in high school, where his drawing teacher encouraged him to pursue a career in illustration, but Evan decided to study biomedical engineering instead, thus ending his formal art education.

He spent his last couple years in high school collaborating on some biotech research, and was eventually published as a coauthor in a scientific journal. He went on to attend Boston University, where he changed majors, and graduated with a B.S. in computer engineering. Evan got back into visual art during college, making pixel-art animations for some computer games that he was developing in his spare time.

After completing his studies, he started a career with a brokerage, writing low latency code for high frequency trading applications. Spending significant time at work dealing with computers, Evan redeveloped an interest in traditional easel painting. He began to refine his painting technique in the evenings after work, by making careful copies of baroque masterworks and impressionist paintings.

Evan chooses his painting subjects from his life and surroundings, preferring to paint small still lifes, self portraits and portraits of family members, and plein air studies near his home in the Atlantic Northeast.
